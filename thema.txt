--[[                Thoranil            ]]--

AIM:
Roguelike, Roleplaying, Dungeoncrawler Minetest Subgame. Takes place almost exclusively in vertically stacked layers of dungeon.

GAMEPLAY PARADIGMS:
    - Playercount between 1 and 4
    - No player experience/leveling system
    - Procedurally Generated (or similar) vertically stacked dungeon realm


INTENDED FEATURES:
    - 21 different trapnodes
    - 9 active baddies
    - Feature-sprinkled dungeons
    - Wholistic atmosphere
    - Varying focus of challenge
    - Adjustable difficulty
